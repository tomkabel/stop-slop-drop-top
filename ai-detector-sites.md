# AI Detector Sites - Comprehensive List

A curated list of AI content detection tools similar to GPTZero, organized by use case and pricing.

---

## General Purpose Detectors

### 1. GPTZero
**Website:** https://gptzero.me/

The original AI detector, created by a Princeton student for educators. Offers free, browser-based detection with sentence-level analysis and visual breakdowns.

- **Best for:** Educators, students
- **Free tier:** Yes, fully free
- **Pricing:** Free

---

### 2. Originality.ai
**Website:** https://originality.ai/

Built for publishers and marketing agencies. Combines AI detection with plagiarism checking, fact-checking, and team dashboards.

- **Best for:** Content agencies, publishers, SEO
- **Free tier:** No (300 words free one-time)
- **Pricing:** Pay-per-scan model

---

### 3. Copyleaks
**Website:** https://copyleaks.com/

Enterprise-grade detector with LMS integration. Offers AI and plagiarism detection across 30+ languages.

- **Best for:** Businesses, educational institutions
- **Free tier:** Yes (25,000 characters one-time)
- **Pricing:** Credit-based system

---

### 4. Winston AI
**Website:** https://gowinston.ai/

Designed for educators with OCR support for scanning handwritten documents and images. Provides PDF reports.

- **Best for:** Teachers, freelancers
- **Free tier:** No (14-day trial)
- **Pricing:** Paid plans

---

### 5. AI Detector
**Website:** https://aidetector.com/

Detects multiple LLMs including ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Includes a humanizer tool to rewrite flagged content.

- **Best for:** Most use cases
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Pricing:** Free with paid features

---

### 6. ZeroGPT
**Website:** https://zerogpt.com/

Free forever alternative with support for 50+ languages. Uses multi-stage detection for GPT-4, Gemini, and Claude.

- **Best for:** ESL users, quick checks
- **Free tier:** Yes, unlimited
- **Pricing:** Free

---

## Academic/Educational

### 7. Turnitin
**Website:** https://www.turnitin.com/

Industry standard for academic integrity with AI detection integrated into plagiarism checking. Used by universities worldwide.

- **Best for:** Universities, institutions
- **Free tier:** No
- **Pricing:** Enterprise license

---

### 8. Paperpal
**Website:** https://paperpal.com/

Academic-focused detector that recognizes formal academic writing as human. Offers sentence-level analysis with three categories: human, human-AI blend, and AI-written.

- **Best for:** Researchers, academics, PhD students
- **Free tier:** Yes (5 scans/day, 1,200 words each)
- **Pricing:** Free tier + paid plans from $25/month

---

### 9. QuillBot AI Detector
**Website:** https://quillbot.com/

Student-friendly detector with sentence-level analysis. Can detect major LLMs including GPT-4o, Claude 3.5, Gemini 1.5, and Llama 3.

- **Best for:** Students, freelancers
- **Free tier:** Yes (10 scans/day, 1,200 words each)
- **Pricing:** Free tier + premium plans

---

### 10. Crossplag
**Website:** https://crossplag.io/

Visual, color-coded detection interface with multilingual support. Great for classroom use.

- **Best for:** Schools, researchers
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Pricing:** Free + paid plans

---

## Business/Enterprise

### 11. Writer.com AI Detector
**Website:** https://writer.com/ai-content-detector/

Integrated into the Writer.com writing suite. Best for quick, one-off checks without switching apps.

- **Best for:** Enterprises, brands
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Pricing:** Included in suite subscription

---

### 12. Sapling
**Website:** https://sapling.ai/

Writing assistant with built-in AI detection. Supports multiple languages and integrates with chat tools.

- **Best for:** Customer support, business teams
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Pricing:** Free tier + upgrades

---

### 13. BrandWell (formerly Content at Scale)
**Website:** https://brandwell.ai/

SEO-oriented detector that checks content originality alongside readability and keyword metrics.

- **Best for:** Marketers, SEO teams
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Pricing:** Free tier + paid

---

## Browser Extensions & Lightweight Tools

### 14. Grammarly AI Detection
**Website:** https://www.grammarly.com/ai-detector

Built directly into the Grammarly editor. Detects AI content while you fix grammar and tone.

- **Best for:** Everyday writers
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Pricing:** Included in Grammarly tiers

---

### 15. GPT Radar
**Website:** https://gptradar.com/

Lightweight, instant online scans for short text samples. No login required.

- **Best for:** Quick checks
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Pricing:** Free

---

### 16. DetectGPT
**Website:** https://detectgpt.com/

Open-source research detector. Measures token variance for transparent, reproducible results.

- **Best for:** Developers, researchers
- **Free tier:** Yes (open source)
- **Pricing:** Free

---

## AI Humanizers (Rewriting Tools)

### 17. WriteBros.ai
**Website:** https://writebros.ai/

AI humanizer that rewrites AI content to sound natural. Helps content pass detection while maintaining meaning.

- **Best for:** Students, marketers, writers
- **Free tier:** Yes (trial available)
- **Pricing:** $1.99/month - $15/month
